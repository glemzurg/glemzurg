# Payment

This domain manages all of the different forms of customer payment, including combinations of payment methods at the same time.

Concepts in this domain include:

- Payment method
- Financial institution
- Credit/debit card
- Gift certificate
- Loyalty points
- Payment authorized
- Payment declined
- Refunds
