# High Availability

This domain makes sure that users can access sytem functionality as often as possible. Specfically, unavailability due to hardware failures needs to be minimized. The focus is on "availability," not "reliability." Reliability means never lsoing data. With availability, if somthing goes down, it neds to get back on the air as quickly as possible, even if it means losign some data in the process. Of course it would be best to lose as little data as possible, but the focus is on being on the air (avaialble)k, not being 100% correct (reliable).

Concepts in this domain include:

- N+M redundancy
- Heartbeat
- Checkpoint
- Failover
